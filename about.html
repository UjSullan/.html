About This Project

"Bunga ng Bayan: A Taste of Philippine Exclusive Fruits" is a digital exhibit highlighting the diverse, rare, and native fruits of the Philippines. This project is a celebration of our agricultural heritage and biodiversity, bringing attention to fruits that are often overlooked in mainstream media and markets.

Through compelling visuals, rich descriptions, and cultural narratives, we aim to inform and inspire a deeper appreciation for these treasures of the tropics. Whether you're a curious foodie, a student, or simply someone proud of our culture, there's something to discover here.
